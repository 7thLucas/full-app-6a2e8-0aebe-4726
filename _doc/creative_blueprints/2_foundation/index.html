The Challenge Behind the Challenge

The Problem

Long-form challenges demand consistency across days or weeks — but without a dedicated tracker, progress is invisible. Check-ins go unrecorded, milestones pass uncelebrated, and participants lose momentum and drop out.

The Solution

Forest Ghost 100 is a focused companion app that logs every session, celebrates every milestone, and keeps the community visible — turning a solitary grind into a shared journey from 0 to 100.
