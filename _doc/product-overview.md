# Forest Ghost 100 — Product Overview

## What It Is
Forest Ghost 100 is a challenge-tracking app for participants of the Forest Ghost 100 challenge — an endurance or outdoor adventure challenge. The app enables participants to log progress, track milestones, maintain streaks, and stay connected with fellow challengers through to completion.

## Users
- **Challengers** (primary): Individual participants enrolled in the Forest Ghost 100 challenge — logging sessions, tracking their 100-unit progress, and earning milestone rewards
- **Organizers** (secondary): Challenge coordinators who monitor overall participation and completion rates

## Positioning
A focused companion app for the Forest Ghost 100 challenge. Removes the friction of manual tracking and keeps participants motivated and accountable from day one to 100.

## Brand & Tone
- **Personality**: Adventurous, earthy, determined, quietly powerful
- **Aesthetic**: Forest-inspired — deep greens, dark earth tones, clean and minimal
- **Voice**: Direct and motivating; no fluff, just forward momentum

## Core Capabilities
- **Progress check-ins**: Participants log each completed session, stage, or mile toward their 100-unit goal
- **Milestone badges**: Earned at 25, 50, 75, and 100 units to mark progress and celebrate achievement
- **Streak tracking**: Consistency streaks reinforce the daily/weekly habit needed to reach 100
- **Leaderboard**: Community ranking to fuel friendly competition and social accountability
- **Completion certificate**: A shareable digital certificate when a participant crosses the 100-unit finish
- **Progress dashboard**: Visual overview of distance covered, days elapsed, and goal remaining

## Strategic Principles
- Every feature must serve the challenger's journey from 0 to 100
- Completion is the primary success metric — everything else supports it
- Community accountability (streaks, leaderboard, shared milestones) reduces drop-off and sustains engagement

## Status
Early concept — product scoping in progress.
